Logos

Usage Guidelines for Liferay Logos

Do not change the color on any part of the Liferay logo.

Do not disassemble the logo to use its parts as graphical elements.

Do not re-create or modify the logo using other typefaces.

Do not place the logo on a background, image or texture that inhibits legibility.

Do not distort, extrude or display the logo as a three-dimensional object.

Do not build logotypes based around logo.

Do not place text or images on top of the logo or beyond its 3x clearspace.

Do not stretch, skew or scale the logo unproportionately.

Do not feather or fade any part of the logo.

Liferay Partners

The Liferay corporate logo is the most visible and recognizable expression of the Liferay brand. We understand and appreciate that our partners may want to show affiliation with Liferay.
